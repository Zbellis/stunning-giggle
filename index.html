About Me

Computing Professional | Leadership & Public Speaker

I am Senior Computer Science major with two internship experiences and I will graduate in May 2021. I am in the Emerging Leader's (EL) Scholarship program, a hand selected group of 30 students out of 250+ applications. I have interest in Mobile App Development, Augmented & Virtual Reality, Leadership & Public Speaking, and writing about Health. I have won $4,000 in scholarship rewards for building iOS mobile app games since I started to learn how to code my freshman year of college.

As an EL I have had the deluge to learn about leadership and apply them in my extra curriculars. Before Code Crew, I was named Orientation Guide of the year for being a standout student and connecting extremely well with parents and students during their first experiences at the University of Memphis.

As a health enthusit I have ran one marathon in 4:14:36 and biked a century (100 miles) in 7:43:00 and maintain a blog about Health on the Strava browser/app.
